About

This Rock Paper Scissors game is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You’ll play against the computer and see who gets the higher score!

How to Play

  • Click on Rock, Paper, or Scissors to make your move.
  • The computer will choose randomly.
  • Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock.
  • Keep track of scores and play as many rounds as you like!

Game Rules

  • Rock > Scissors
  • Scissors > Paper
  • Paper > Rock
  • Use the Restart button to reset the score anytime.
